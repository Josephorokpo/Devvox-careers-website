About Us

Welcome to our about page! Meet the team behind Devvox Careers.

Joseph Orokpo and Burhanuddeen Abdullahi are students of the Software Engineering (SE) programme by ALX Africa, a twelve-month intensive software engineering programme. The development of the Devvox Careers Website was initiated as a portfolio project within the ALX SE programme.

The idea to build this project was born out of the evident challenges faced by tech graduates in securing job opportunities. With a shared determination to address this issue, Joseph and Burhanuddeen embarked on developing the Devvox Careers Website as a solution to simplify the job search process for tech professionals.
